Key Milestones in Digitalization

2000s: Dot-Com Boom and Social Media

The 2000s witnessed the rise of e-commerce, search engines, and social media platforms that transformed how people interact online.

E-Commerce

  • Amazon (1994)
  • eBay (1995)
  • PayPal (1998)

Search & Info

  • Google (1998)
  • Wikipedia (2001)
  • YouTube (2005)

Social Media

  • MySpace (2003)
  • Facebook (2004)
  • Twitter (2006)

2010s: Mobile Internet and Cloud Computing

The 2010s saw the proliferation of smartphones and cloud computing, revolutionizing how people access and store data.

Mobile Revolution

  • iPhone (2007)
  • Android (2008)
  • App Store (2008)
  • Responsive Design
  • PWAs (2015)

Cloud Computing

  • AWS (2006)
  • Azure (2010)
  • Google Cloud (2011)

2020s: AI, IoT, and Web3

The 2020s are defined by advancements in Artificial Intelligence (AI), the Internet of Things (IoT), and the decentralized Web3 ecosystem.

AI/ML

  • GPT models (2018+)
  • Computer vision
  • Voice assistants

IoT

  • Smart homes
  • Wearables
  • Industrial IoT

Web3

  • Blockchain
  • Cryptocurrencies
  • NFTs
